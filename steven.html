Steven's
Journey

Hey there! First of all, thank you for stoping by our website and checking out our #75Hard journey.
About Me: My Name is Steven, I'm 25 years old, and I work full time as a cybersecurity engineer. I grew up playing sports which is where I developed my passion for fitness at all levels.
Why #75Hard: I have taken 1stPhorm products for a while and listen to Andy Frisella talk on many occasions, and when I heard about the #75Hard challenge, I KNEW this was something I had to accomplish. Forming a cohort with a group of friends that all want to push themselves to the next level by taking on this challenge was by far the best decisions we have ever made.
My Goals: I want to become my absolute best. I believe this program will teach me a lot about myself, and I want to utilize the things I learn during this process to become a better, stronger, more focused, more dedicated, and more resilient version of myself. Along with this, I want to inspire people to be their best and to not settle for mediocrity in their lives.
My Books: 75 HARD by Andy Frisella, Can't Hurt Me by David Goggins, MAKE YOUR BED by Admiral William H. McRaven, Start with Why by Simon Sinek
Meal Plan: 3,478 calories. Macros -- Protein: 349g (39%), Carbs: 311g (35%), Fat: 103g (26%)
